5.2 Control Plan

The Flick team will be using a variety of softwares to maintain control and visibility of project milestones. Outlined below is a breakdown of our control plan.

Quality:

Git will be used for version control. See section 7.3 to see our full quality assurance plan.

Reporting and Requirements:

Discord & Facebook messenger will be used for communication. Group meetings will be held every Tuesday & Thursday for status updates, change requests, and review. Meeting notes and next steps will also be documented using Google Docs.

Schedule

Trello will be used for project management. Google Sheets will be used to track individual contributions and hours worked.
